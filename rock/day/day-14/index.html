Janis Joplin - Little Girl Blue - I Got Dem Ol' Kozmic Blues Again Mama! - 1969

原作者为Richard Rodgers，歌词作者为Lorenz Hart。这份录音被收录在Janis Joplins离开Big Brother and Holding Company后的第一张专辑里。

Janis的歌声有强大的感染力，仿佛心浸泡在歌声中。从歌声中，心感受到了平静，珍惜和美好。

Original writer is Richard Rodgers, lyrics written by Lorenz Hart. This recording is by Janis Joplin. She recorded this in her first album after leaving Big Brother and Holding Company.
