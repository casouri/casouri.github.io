You open the door and step into a room. A late-morning sun radiates through a large window across the room. Your skin feels the warm touch of its rays. Your heart floats like a buoy risen from deep water—the sunlight soaks your body with joy and energy. Without second though, you take a deep breath, and exhale out. A pleasent scents of herb and wood brushes though your nostril.

You meander towards the window, and the table in front catches your eye. It’s a large wooden table of robust construction. On top of it is a pile of loose scrolls, opened tomes, and scraps of paper that’s on the verge of spilling over. Inklings of numerous color and script cover the pages. Each piece seems to be in a different language, most of which completely alien to you. Fortunately, you can make out a few of them. Curiosly, you pick up one that reads:

☞ Notes
☞ 余日摇滚
☞ Your Next Stop
☞ 超喜欢
