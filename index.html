You open the door and step into a room. A near-noon sun radiates through a large window across the room. Your skin feels the warm touch of its rays. Your heart floats like a buoy risen from deep water—the sunlight almost radiates joy and energy in to your body. Without second though, you take a deep breath, and exhale out. A pleasent scents of herbs and wood went though your nose.

You meander towards the window, basking in the sun. Before you look out the window, the table in front catches your eye. It’s a large wooden table of robust construction and a health old age. You glance through the pile of loose scrolls, opened tomes, and scraps of paper that cover up most of the table. Inklings of numerous color and script crowds the pages and pieces. Each piece seems to be in a different language, most of which completely alien to you. Fortunately, you can make out a few of them. Out of curiosity, you pick up the one that reads:

☞ Notes
☞ 余日摇滚
☞ Your Next Stop
☞ 珍宝
